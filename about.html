About Aplimac

Aplimac is a Zimbabwean based firm. We do custom software development, complete software development outsourcing, software product development, Software consulting services and software support and evolution. Having started so vibrant and full of innovation we dedicated the rest of our years adopting new technology and ever since we have helped I.T and non-IT organizations and also software product companies to improve their business performance and quickly win new customers.

We can develop applications suiting your needs. After we do an analysis we will automate your business process. When an organization has a specific software need that can't be addressed with our off-the-shelf software, it commissions our developers to create custom software that suits or rather address the organisation’s specific needs.

We also partner with other companies to come up with integrated API platforms. Therefore, our solutions are not limited. This means that our products are scalable through the integration of other systems’ APIs. We can integrate APIs or do the other way round, we also offer APIs to systems that need our service modules.

We have built up expertise in CRM, ERP, Ecommerce, Data Analytics, Information Security, DMS, and other areas and conquered such innovative fields as Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Big Data and Internet of Things

We continue to grow and additionally have a partner network of companies, which, combined with our strong technological expertise, enables us to cover all development and infrastructure needs of midsized and large organizations.
